About

We are affiliated to Toastmasters International, a world leader in helping people become more competent and comfortable in front of an audience. The nonprofit organization now has nearly 357,000 members in more than 16,000 clubs in 143 countries, offering a proven – and enjoyable! – way to practice and hone communication and leadership skills.

Gurgaon Toastmasters club is the premier Toastmasters club in Gurgaon. It was chartered on 25th June 2008 and has been a proud recipient of many accolades and awards since its inception

Toastmasters International Mission

We empower individuals to become more effective communicators and leaders

Club Mission

We provide a supportive and positive learning experience in which members are empowered to develop communication and leadership skills, resulting in greater self-confidence and personal growth.

Values & Promise

Our core Values are - Integrity, Respect, Service and Excellence. We promise to empower individuals through personal and professional development

President

"You get in life what you have the courage to ask for" A saying that has stayed with me for a long time. We are often de-motivated by thoughts of what others might think of us, what they might say and how we might embarass ourselves that we do not even try to achieve somethings we desire deeply. But what we don't realize is that maybe everyone is going through the same thing. In this extremely competitive cut-throat world, maybe everyone is a little afraid of failure, of taking the wrong path. So when we take a step towards our dreams, people might not like it, they might make fun of it, they might try to pull us down but they can only do that if we let them. Toastmasters is THE most supportive community, a safe place, full of energetic enthusiastic and positive individuals from diverse backgrounds who are willing to break the barriers and help each other in their journey of personal growth. Toastmasters is an experience in itself which cannot be described in just a few words.

What does a toastmasters meeting entail?

We at Gurgaon toastmasters have a themed meetings. Each week a new theme is introduced about which a toastmaster talks in brief. Each meeting is divided into 3 sections - Prepared speeches, Table Topics and evaluations. It is a two hour meeting, wherein each member takes up a different role. We encourage guests to participate in the table topics section of the meeting.

Meeting Etiquettes -

  • We start the meetings sharp at the given time, so don't be late.
  • If you are late, enter only during applause.
  • Keep your cellphones on silent.
  • Be quiet and respectful.
  • Keep the enthusiasm up by clapping everytime someone is introduced on the stage!
